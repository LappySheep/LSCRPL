Function = add

Format:

a b add

Purpose:

(Little Endian) a+b
(Big Endian) b+a

Pops result on to the stack

Examples:
(Little Endian)

3 4 add 2 1 add add
7 2 1 add add
9 1 add
10

(Big Endian)

3 4 add 2 1 add add

7 2 1 add add
7 3 add
10


If the add function is used with arrays...

#little endian for readability
1 2 3 4 5 5 arr{a}
2 3 4 5 6 5 arr{b}

arr{a} arr{b} add ->c
<-c !out

>> 1 2 3 4 5 2 3 4 5 6
